Pedro

Ambition

With grease-stained hands and a heart so bold,
I’ll chase my dreams as stories unfold.
A wrench in my pocket, the road in my sight,
Fixing engines by day, chasing stars at night.

From city streets to desert sands,
I’ll mend the wheels with steady hands.
Roaring motors, humming tunes,
Under blazing suns and silver moons.

In Tokyo’s glow or Rome’s old ways,
On highways long or mountain bays,
Every engine tells a tale,
Of distant lands and whispered trails.

No place too far, no road too steep,
With roaring hearts, our dreams we keep.
For every mile and bolt I turn,
A piece of life I’ll always learn.
